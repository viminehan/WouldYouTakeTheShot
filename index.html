About the Simulation

As a military sniper, you have to make hard decisions with limited information.

You will be given scenarios and you will need to decide if you shoot your target, or hold your fire.

Upon completion, you will be shown an analysis of your decisions.

Warning: This game may disturb some players. If you experience PTSD or are concerned, do not play.
